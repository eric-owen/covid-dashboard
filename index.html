Welcome and thank you for using our Covid Dashboard, the place where we provide you with the latest COVID information worldwide! How to use the Covid Dashboard? It's really simple, just follow the 3 easy steps under "Instructions" and you're all set to go! We hope you'll have a wonderful experience with our Covid Dashboard, enjoy!


Instructions:

1. To start, select a state under Get Heat Map! Once a state is selected, a terrain view of that state will display on the map below.
2. Then press the Toggle Map button to get a heat map view of all areas affected by COVID on your selected state. You can then alter the display of your heat map using the other buttons.
3. View your results! The stat results will display on the right under "Stat results".
