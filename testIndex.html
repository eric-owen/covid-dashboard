Welcome to the Covid Tracker!

Thank you for visiting our Covid Tracker site, the place where we provide you with the most recent COVID information worldwide! How to use the Covid Tracker? It's simple, just follow the 3 easy steps under "Instructions" and you're all set to go! We hope you'll have a wonderful experience with our Covid Tracker, enjoy!

Instructions:

1. To start, select a state under "Get Heat Map!" Once a state is selected, a terrain view of that state will display on the map below.

2. Then press Toggle Map to get a heat map view of all areas affected by COVID on your selected state. You can also change the display of your heat map.

3. View your results! The stat results will display on the right under "Stat results".
